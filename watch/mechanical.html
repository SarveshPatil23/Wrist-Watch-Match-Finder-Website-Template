MECHANICAL WATCHES

Mechanical wristwatches are timepieces that rely on intricate mechanical movements to measure time. They consist of a mainspring, balance wheel, and escapement mechanism to regulate the release of energy, resulting in the precise movement of hands on the watch dial. These watches are often prized for their craftsmanship, with intricate designs and transparent case backs that showcase the intricate mechanisms. Unlike quartz watches, they require manual winding or, in the case of automatic watches, they wind themselves through the wearer's movement. Mechanical watches are admired for their timeless elegance and the artistry that goes into their creation.
